# Virtuous Cycle — Brand Guidelines

**Canonical reference · v2 (2026) · VC-BRAND-2026-02**
Single source of truth. Where older artifacts disagree, this document and `tokens.json` govern. This supersedes the earlier **olive (#5F8604)** system — the brand green is now **Verdant #6E9E10**.

> This file is written to be read by both a human designer and a language model. Paste it (with `tokens.json`) into any model to brief it on the brand.

## 1. Essence & positioning

- **Essence:** *Land, livelihood, and the common good.*
- **Tagline (the line we lead with):** *People-powered. Place-rooted. Purpose-driven.* The hero line — sets the tone across website and social.
- **Capability line (states the offer, pairs under the tagline):** *PPP + Development Advisory for financeable, durable projects.* Scales from advisory through principal, GP / co-GP, JV, and SPV roles. The wordmark itself stays descriptor-free.
- **Signature line (quiet, recurring):** *Land, livelihood & the common good.* The essence, used as the line that recurs on collateral mastheads, signatures, and the site footer.
- **Motto (story framing):** *Development that compounds.* The virtuous-cycle idea said plainly — for section intros and value framing.
- **One-liner:** Virtuous Cycle is a mission-driven development and advisory firm — helping communities grow with integrity by aligning design, investment, and strategy.

**Who we serve:** dioceses & institutional landowners, municipalities, developers, attorneys, civil engineers, and finance leads making high-stakes decisions about land.

**The impression we must make:** *"This firm thinks clearly, writes well, and takes my time seriously."* Command shows through economy of language, not volume.

**We are not:** a startup, a law firm, a nonprofit, or a generic consulting shop.

**Retired language:** "Development advisory for the common good" (states the service, not the firm — reads like a directory listing), "Real estate with purpose / strategy with soul" (too soft for the audience), and "place-strengthening" (clunky). The capability line is fixed at *"PPP + Development Advisory for financeable, durable projects"* — do not revert it to any earlier wording.

## 3. The logo

A woven **Celtic love-knot** — four loops returning to their source, carrying the "virtuous cycle" idea before a word is read.

- **Primary (stacked):** `assets/logo-verdant.png`
- **Mark / favicon:** `assets/icon-verdant.png`, `assets/favicon.png`
- **Reversed:** white mark on Pine/Field (CSS `filter: brightness(0) invert(1)` on the verdant mark, or a dedicated white asset).
- **Color:** Verdant `#6E9E10` on light; white on dark green.
- **Clear space:** ≥ *x* on all sides, where *x* = the height of one loop of the mark.
- **Minimum size:** 32 px on screen · 0.5 in in print.

**Never:** recolor or place on an off-brand color · stretch, squash, or rotate · add gradients, shadows, or a containing box.

## 4. Color — one hue, a family of tones

Color is hierarchy, not decoration. A single verdant hue carries identity; lighter and deeper tones of the **same hue** do the supporting work. **No off-family color.** Warmth comes from imagery (the watercolor + photography), never a second swatch.

### Green family
| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Mist | `#EEF3E0` | tints, table fills, quiet panels |
| Sage | `#CFE0A8` | rules, soft dividers, ribbon mid-tones |
| Leaf | `#9DC65A` | diagram fills, secondary bars, charts |
| **Verdant (anchor)** | **`#6E9E10`** | identity, accents, kickers, lead bars, fills |
| Moss | `#4F7510` | **green text on white** (legible), hovers, 2nd-level bars |
| Pine | `#2F4A0C` | panels, dividers, depth |
| Field | `#16230A` | covers, dark grounds, reversed type |

### Neutrals (warm, green-cast)
| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Ink | `#1A2230` | display & body text |
| Grey | `#555555` | captions, sources, metadata |
| Grey Light | `#8A9384` | faint labels, folios |
| Light Fill | `#F4F6F1` | warm house off-white (never blue-grey) |
| Hair | `#E3E7DE` | hairline borders |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | page ground, reversed text |

**Rules**
- Accent at most one phrase per heading in Verdant.
- For small green text on white, use **Moss `#4F7510`**, not Verdant — contrast.
- Never set green text on a green field.
- Callouts separate by **weight and rule, not hue**: Verdant = Key Finding · Ink = Risk · Moss = Recommendation.
- **Retired:** amber `#C88A2B` and client navy `#2E4A6B` (off-family).

## 5. Typography — two families

**Spectral speaks and reflects · Inter explains.** One system across web and documents. In Office, substitute **Georgia** (serif) and **Arial** (sans).

- **Spectral** (serif): display, titles, lede, big numbers, and the one reflective *italic* line that closes a page.
- **Inter** (sans): body at 1.6 line-height, labels, captions, UI, data. SemiBold only for the first words of a paragraph (inline subhead) or tracked caps labels — never mid-sentence emphasis.

Google Fonts: `Spectral:ital,wght@0,400;0,500;0,600;0,700;1,400;1,500;1,600` + `Inter:wght@400;500;600;700`.

| Role | Family / weight | Size |
|---|---|---|
| Cover title | Spectral 600 | 64px |
| Page title | Spectral 600 | 40px (33px small) |
| Lede | Spectral 500 | 16px (Pine) |
| Takeaway | Spectral 500 italic | 15px, left green rule |
| Body | Inter 400 | 12.5px / 1.62 |
| Kicker / Label | Inter 600 caps, tracked | 10px / 9px (Moss) |

Minimums: print body ≥ 12px; slide text ≥ 24px. One reflective italic line per page, maximum.

## 6. Voice & signature language

Warm, clear, convicted. Accessible language. Lift up people and place. **Authority comes from economy, not volume.**

**Do:** "We help communities reimagine what's possible." · "We build with, not for." · "The street typology decision is worth $6–17M."

**Don't:** "We deliver comprehensive revitalization solutions." · "We execute stakeholder engagement protocols." · "It is worth noting that value may be created."

**Signature phrases (use consistently):** The Virtuous Cycle Process · People-powered. Place-rooted. Purpose-driven. · Land, livelihood & the common good · Development that compounds · Place-based strategy · Natural and human ecosystems · Regenerative development · With, not for · Stewardship, not just development.

**Banned:** jargon, corporate clichés, exclamation marks, second person in formal deliverables, "place-strengthening," soul-softness. (The approved tagline "Purpose-driven" is the one sanctioned use of "purpose" — a tracked triad, never loose "purpose-driven solutions" filler.)

## 7. The Virtuous Cycle Process

A proprietary five-phase cycle that guides every engagement — honoring people, place, and purpose, and returning to its source stronger.

1. **Root** — *Listen & Locate.* Begin in the place; surface objectives, constraints, concrete facts.
2. **Align** — *Align & Assess.* Reconcile interests; test feasibility against instruments that exist.
3. **Shape** — *Design & Develop.* Translate alignment into a structured plan, terms, and value-carrying decisions.
4. **Weave** — *Build & Belong.* Deliver with the community so the outcome belongs to the place.
5. **Return** — *Steward & Sustain.* Steward what was built; let value compound and feed the next turn.

## 8. The ribbon motif

Layered green-family bands flowing and returning — the cycle, abstracted (`assets/ribbon-v2.svg`). **Covers and section dividers only**, one signature element per page. Never on data pages, never behind body text, never recolored outside the family.

---

## 9. Document anatomy

Every deliverable carries the same skeleton:
1. **Header band** — Verdant 56px: mark + wordmark left, document section right.
2. **Lede** — one Spectral sentence states the argument; the body proves it.
3. **Exhibits** — titles written as findings, not subjects; **ranges, never point estimates**; sources in grey.
4. **Takeaway** — one reflective italic line.
5. **Footer (every page)** — `Doc code · title — Virtuous Cycle LLC · Confidential · Not an appraisal`, page number right.

Doc codes: `VC-[client]-[year]-NN`, sequential per engagement.

## 10. Imagery

Warmth and place come from **a mix of original watercolor illustration** (in the Pensacola Bay style of the current hero) **and real photography** of places and people — not from color. Keep palette to the green family + neutrals; let the image supply the heat. Commission new watercolors in the same hand for hero moments; use restrained, real photography elsewhere.
