4Type — two families

Spectral
Speaks & reflects
Display, titles, lede, big numbers, and the one reflective italic line that closes a page. Weights 500–700. Office fallback: Georgia.
Inter
Explains
Body at 1.6 line-height, labels, captions, UI, data. SemiBold only for lead-in words or tracked-caps labels — never mid-sentence emphasis. Office fallback: Arial.

5The logo

A woven Celtic knot — four loops returning to their source. Verdant on light, white on Pine/Field. Clear space ≥ one loop; never below 32 px on screen or 0.5 in in print.

Never
  • Recolor the mark or place it on an off-brand color.
  • Stretch, squash, or rotate it.
  • Add gradients, shadows, or a containing box.

6Voice

Warm, clear, convicted. Authority through economy, not volume.

Do

  • “We help communities reimagine what's possible.”
  • “We build with, not for.”
  • “The street typology decision is worth $6–17M.”

Don't

  • “We deliver comprehensive revitalization solutions.”
  • “We execute stakeholder engagement protocols.”
  • “It is worth noting that value may be created.”
Non-negotiables
  • One signature element per page (a band, a watercolor, or the ribbon — never two).
  • Findings in ranges, never point estimates ($6–17M, not $11M).
  • Sections separate by weight and rule, not by adding hues.
  • No jargon, corporate clichés, exclamation marks, or the retired words: “place-strengthening,” “strategy with soul.”

7Submitting work
