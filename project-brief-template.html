Brand — non-negotiable

These govern every deliverable. Source: BRAND.md · tokens.json · handoff page
  • Color: one verdant hue + its family (Verdant #6E9E10 anchor). No off-family color — warmth comes from imagery, not a second swatch. Small green text on white uses Moss #4F7510.
  • Type: Spectral (headings/serif) + Inter (body/sans). Office: Georgia + Arial.
  • Logo: Verdant on light, white on Pine/Field. Clear space ≥ one loop; min 32px screen / 0.5in print. Never recolor, stretch, or box it.
  • Layout: one signature element per page; separate sections by weight & rule, not hue. Restraint is the brand.
  • Voice: warm, clear, convicted. No jargon, clichés, exclamation marks, or the retired words (“place-strengthening,” “strategy with soul”).
